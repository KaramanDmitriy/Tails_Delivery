About us

About us

Our team "Tails Delivery" provides transportation of pets within Ukraine.

We work with partners for delivery to the countries of the European Union (EU).

We have many years of experience in the transportation of animals. We do it carefully and reliably. During transportation, we take care of the physical condition and good mood of the pets.

We provide constant support with photos and videos so that you can be sure of the safety and comfort of your pet during transportation. We will feed, water and walk your pet during a long trip.
